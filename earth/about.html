About Earth Masia

Learn about our mission, vision, values, and the team behind our success in OHS and Environmental consulting.

Our Story

Earth Masia (PTY) LTD was founded with a clear mission: to empower organizations across South Africa with practical, cost-effective safety and sustainability expertise. We believe that every workplace should be safe, healthy, and environmentally responsible.

With over a decade of industry experience, our team of certified professionals has helped numerous organizations across various sectors achieve and maintain the highest standards of safety and environmental stewardship.

We combine technical expertise with practical solutions, ensuring that our clients not only meet regulatory requirements but also build sustainable, resilient operations that protect both people and the planet.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with practical, innovative safety and environmental solutions that minimize risk, ensure compliance, and promote sustainable operations that protect both people and the planet.

Our Vision

To be the leading OHS and Environmental consulting firm in South Africa, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and tangible results in creating safer, more sustainable workplaces.

Our Values

Safety First

Collaboration
